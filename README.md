# BioMed-KG — Biomedical Knowledge Graph & RAG System

A biomedical knowledge graph integrating multi-layered genomic and clinical data to reveal 
complex relationships among SNPs, genes, biomarkers, diseases, and drugs — enhanced with 
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Large Language Models (LLMs).

> Research Internship — KAUST Smart Health Initiative (SSI Program)  
> Supervised by Prof. Xin Gao | King Abdullah University of Science and Technology

🏆 Awarded 3rd Place — Best Project & ePoster Design among all SSI interns

---

## The Problem

Genomic and clinical data is scattered across thousands of publications and databases, making 
it extremely difficult for researchers and clinicians to discover biomarkers, understand disease 
mechanisms, and advance precision medicine.

---

## What BioMed-KG Does

BioMed-KG addresses this by building a unified knowledge graph that:

- Connects SNPs, genes, biomarkers, diseases, and drugs into a single queryable structure
- Reveals complex biological relationships hidden across large-scale datasets
- Provides AI-driven explanations to help clinicians uncover disease mechanisms and causal pathways
- Integrates RAG with LLMs to enable intelligent, data-driven scientific discovery
